Living Type

Living Type is a Brisbane based rock trio connecting audiences with a healthy dose of originals and covers.

The band have recorded two EPs over the last few years as well as entertaining live crowds with songs that take you from "Hey, I know this song" to where you can't help but sing along.

Enjoy songs ranging from intimate acoustic ballads to rocking foot stompers that end with band members dancing on bars. Come and get amongst it!
